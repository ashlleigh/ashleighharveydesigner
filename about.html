About Me

Experienced Designer with a demonstrated history of working in the digital marketing industry, and skilled across print and digital design. I am currently freelancing while seeking full-time work.

I started studying interactive design at University in 2014, however I changed to a Business Degree majoring in Advertising and Grapic Design. I have now ironically fallen back in love with interaction design through discovering UX/UI design through work and personal research.
